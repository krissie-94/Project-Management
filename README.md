# Project Title: Enrich NGO Website Redesign
Project Duration: 10 Weeks
Project Goal: Redesign Enrich NGO’s website to improve accessibility, increase donor engagement,
and streamline online program registration. The redesigned site aims to increase donation conversions
by 20% and improve overall user experience.
# Project Scope
In Scope:
- Redesign of homepage, About page, Donate page, Program pages, and Contact section
- Creation of new UI/UX mockups following accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1)
- Integration of improved donation flow (simplified forms and confirmation emails)
- SEO improvements (metadata, alt-text, speed optimization)
- User testing with donors, volunteers, and beneficiaries
- Migration of content to new design templates
# Out of Scope:
- Rebranding or new logo creation
- Major backend CMS changes
- New mobile app development
# Deliverables
- Website requirements document
- UI/UX wireframes and final design
- Updated responsive web pages
- Donation workflow redesign
- SEO and accessibility audit report
- User testing report
- Final deployed website & project closure summary
# Milestones
- Week 2: Requirements finalized
- Week 3: UI/UX mockups approved by NGO board
- Week 6: Development completed
- Week 7: Accessibility and SEO testing completed
- Week 8: User testing completed
- Week 10: Website launch and project closure

# Community Back-to-School Campaign – Project Plan
# & Gantt Chart
Project Title: Community Back-to-School Campaign
Project Duration: 12 Weeks
Project Goal: Organize a community-wide back-to-school event providing school supplies, health
screenings, and educational workshops to support 500+ students and families before the new school
year.
# Project Scope
In Scope:
- Distribution of 500–700 backpacks and school supply kits
- Organizing 10 community partner booths (libraries, health orgs, tutoring centers)
- Free health screenings (vision, dental, general wellness)
- Volunteer recruitment and training
- Event marketing (flyers, social media campaign, school newsletter placement)
- Post-event survey and impact report
# Out of Scope:
- Financial aid services
- Long-term tutoring programs
- Non-local vendor partnerships
# Deliverables
- Finalized event plan
- Confirmed venue & vendor contracts
- Marketing materials (flyer, social media graphics, press release)
- Volunteer roster & training guide
- Distribution station layouts & logistics map
- Post-event survey results
- Final impact summary report
- Milestones
- Week 2: Venue and vendor approvals completed
- Week 4: Marketing campaign launches
- Week 6: Volunteers recruited and scheduled
- Week 8: Supplies purchased and inventory completed
- Week 10: Final event walkthrough with partners
- Week 12: Event day and post-event reporting completed
# Risk Register
Risk Impact Likelihood Mitigation
-----------------------------------------------------------------------
Low volunteer turnout High Medium Recruit early; offer incentives
Vendor cancellation High Low Identify backup vendors
Supply shipment delays High Medium Order supplies 3 weeks early
Low event turnout Medium Medium Increase marketing & school partnerships
Weather issues (outdoor venue) Medium Medium Secure indoor backup location
# Communication Plan
- Weekly check-ins with community partners
- Bi-weekly project status emails to stakeholders
- Monday/Wednesday volunteer coordination updates
- Slack channel for real-time updates
